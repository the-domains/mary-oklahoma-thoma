VIRGIN VIDEO PRODUCTION…THAT’S RIGHT MY VERY FIRST TIME.

"Below the Surface" Art Demonstration Video 2 6 2016

Mary Thoma
MaryOklahomaThoma, guest designer for StencilGirl Products blog "Stencil Talk". Publication 2-12-2016. I am the artist, videographer and editor. Enjoy!

Below the Surface: My very first video ever, recorded, edited and staring your’s truly, MaryOklahomaThoma. I am not crazy about how the artwork turned out but I really like the video final outcome. I felt good about it for my VIRGIN VIDEO run. It certainly made me aware of what I like and don’t like about the process I used. Just like all fledgling film makers, the learning curve is under negotiation. Feedback is always helpful, plus you could like, subscribe and share. I am developing a “video studio” and my video creations will start airing on a regular basis this fall.
